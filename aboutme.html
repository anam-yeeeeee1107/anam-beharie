Heyyy!! I'm Anam Beharie, aged 15 and born on July 1st, 2009. I am originally from Durban, South Africa but have Indian ancestors. This is my story... When I was just at the mere age of 4, I immigrated to New Zealand and stayed with my aunt and her family for a couple of years. Although most my of family was back in South Africa, I'm still very close with them. For 6 months I went to the kindy down the road from my aunt's and when I turned 5, I went to Point View School and Somerville Intermediate before starting at BDSC, which is where I'm at now. I guess it wasn't so bad moving to NZ, after all I had some of my cousins, aunties, uncles and closest friends here who I am very grateful for, guiding and supporting me through my highs and lows in life. Ever since I was a little girl there were five things I wanted in my life forever: happiness; love; my family and my best friends; a good education and career; and lastly, to travel the world!
